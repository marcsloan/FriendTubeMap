Putting your Facebook friends on the London Underground

Ever wanted to see what your social network would look like as a tube map? Get started by following the steps below to grab your social network data.

  1. Head over to the NetGet website (try opening it in another tab so you can still see these instructions)
  2. Give NetGet permission to access your Facebook profile so it can grab your friend list
  3. Once the page loads, leave all the checkboxes empty and click the here link in Step 2
  4. Wait for GetNet to finish collecting your social data, this can take a few minutes
  5. Once it's complete, right click on gml file and click 'Save As' to save your social data onto your computer
  6. Upload your social data by clicking the button below!
